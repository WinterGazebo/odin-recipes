Noah's Beer Can Chicken

Beer Can Chicken

  • 1 12 oz can
  • 1 whole uncooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup of any vegetable oil
  • You could use a number of different things to add flavor to your chicken. I use:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Thyme
    • Garlic
    • Onion powder
    • Paprika

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Remove the innards from the chicken.
  3. Empty out half of the soda can and take off the tab then you will slide this into the bottom of the chicken so the can can sit straight up. Put the chicken and can on the sheet ready to go into the oven.
  4. Mix all your desired spices into a bowl with your vegetable oil.
  5. Lather the oil and spices onto the chicken.
  6. Cook it for an hour and forty five minutes or until the inside is 165 degrees fahrenheit.
