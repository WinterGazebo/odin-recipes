Noah's Zesty Chicken Breast

Zesty Chicken Breast

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • Cumin
  • Onion powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Thyme
  • Lemon Juice
A note: I put the most important spices at the top and worked my way down.

  1. Season your chicken and follow your heart.
  2. Put pan on a medium high stove top and add a little bit of oil.
  3. Grill both sides for 6 minutes.
  4. Serve with broccoli.
