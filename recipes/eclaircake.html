Noah's Eclair Cake

Eclair Cake

  • 1 9x13 cake pan
  • 2 3.5 oz packages of instant French vanilla pudding
  • 3 Cups of milk
  • 1 box of graham crackers
  • 8 oz Cool Whip
  • 1 Can of chocolate cake icing

  1. Beat pudding with milk, fold in cool whip
  2. Layer cake pan with graham crackers on the bottom then half of the pudding
  3. Layer more graham crackers and the rest of the pudding and finish with graham crackers on top
  4. Melt and spread chocolate icing on top
  5. Refrigerate overnight
